Delete Your Account and Data

You can request deletion of your Vocurso account and all associated data at any time. Two methods are available — pick whichever is more convenient.

Method 1 — In-app (recommended)

  1. Open the Vocurso app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Data Management → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm. Your account is scheduled for deletion with a 14-day grace period (you can cancel within that window by signing back in).
  4. After the grace period, all data tied to your account is permanently purged from our systems.

Method 2 — Email request

If you cannot access the app (lost device, uninstalled, account locked), email us:

  • To: hoonblizz@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account deletion request
  • Include the email address used for the account so we can verify ownership.

We will confirm receipt within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days of verifying your identity (per GDPR Article 12.3 / Korean PIPA).

What gets deleted

  • Your user record (email, display name, preferred language, subscription status)
  • All career goals, diagnoses, roadmaps, and goal context
  • All practice sessions, interview questions, audio recordings, and transcripts
  • All résumé uploads (PDFs, DOCX, images) and extracted text
  • Any onboarding intent and questionnaire responses

What gets retained (anonymised)

  • Aggregate analytics events are retained with the user identifier set to NULL. The events themselves cannot be linked back to you, and they help us understand product-level usage patterns. This complies with GDPR Recital 26 (anonymous information is not personal data).
  • Subscription records required by tax/accounting law (kept for up to 7 years per local regulations) are retained in aggregate form only.

Subscription cancellation

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active App Store / Play Store subscription. Cancel separately through the relevant store before requesting deletion if you want to avoid further charges. The Vocurso app surfaces a "Manage subscription" deep link in Settings.

Need help?

Email hoonblizz@gmail.com. We respond within 7 business days. See also the full Privacy Policy §9.
